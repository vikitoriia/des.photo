About

Hi, I'm Lilya. Photographer, programmer, and more. As a child, everyone dreamed of becoming an astronaut, a doctor, a police officer, a model, and many other unique personalities, not like everyone else. So I dreamed of becoming a photographer since childhood! I soon learned about the author's photography courses, which I went to without hesitation without sparing money, effort and time. It was the courses that turned photography into my s trange hobby, or rather into life!

I think you, like me, know very well that all beautiful moments are fleeting:
-meeting with a loved one whom you have not seen for a long time;
-the long-awaited birthday present;
-walk with dear friends;
-and many other moments ...

You will never forget such moments, but sometimes they are difficult to remember in detail.

I know you're here for a purpose - because you believe (as I do) that photography is valuable. It can give depth and significance to life. Not only today, but years later. At some point you will want to get photos that sincerely convey the pleasure and love that permeated the past. Photos that you can show to your grandchildren, and the last - to your grandchildren.

You would not want such photos to be created by a person who simply "takes pictures". You want to see the present in all its glory, to capture all the beauty in the current moment. Because tomorrow everything will be different.

The future will bring changes, a new stage will begin, a different story. And the "present" will become "the past", as "today" will turn into "yesterday", "last week", "last month", "last year".
